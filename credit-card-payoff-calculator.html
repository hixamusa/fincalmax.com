📖 What These Results Mean

Cap rate measures a property's return based purely on its income and price, ignoring financing — which makes it the standard way investors compare deals side by side. Enter the price, rental income, and operating expenses (everything except the mortgage) to get your cap rate. Most stable rental markets see cap rates between 4-10%.
