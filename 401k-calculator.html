How Does This Calculator Work?

What Is Included?

This calculator includes Principal & Interest, Property Taxes, Homeowner's Insurance, and optionally PMI — giving you a true monthly cost, not just the loan payment.

2026 Average Rates

Average 30-year fixed rates are around 6.5%–7.2% in 2026. Borrowers with credit scores above 760 typically qualify for the best rates available.

How to Lower Your Payment

Put more down to reduce loan size, improve your credit score for a better rate, or choose a 30-year term for lower monthly payments.

Fixed vs. ARM

Fixed rates never change — great for long-term owners. ARM rates start lower but adjust after 5–7 years. Best for buyers who plan to move or refinance early.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much house can I afford? +
Use the 28/36 rule: spend no more than 28% of gross monthly income on housing costs. Use our Affordability Calculator for a precise number.
What is PMI? +
Private Mortgage Insurance is required when your down payment is under 20%. Use our PMI Calculator to estimate the cost.
15-year or 30-year mortgage? +
A 15-year saves ~40% in total interest but has higher monthly payments. A 30-year gives more cash flow flexibility. Read our full comparison guide.
Should I refinance my mortgage? +
If your new rate is at least 0.75% lower and you plan to stay 3+ years, refinancing likely makes sense. Use our Refinance Calculator to find out.
