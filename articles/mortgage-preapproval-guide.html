Mortgage Pre-Approval vs Pre-Qualification: What Is the Difference?

Sellers see dozens of offers. Pre-approval versus pre-qualification can be the difference between getting your dream home and losing it.

Advertisement · 728×90

Pre-Qualification: Quick but Weak

An informal estimate based on self-reported info. No documents, no credit check, takes 5 minutes. Not a commitment from the lender.

Pre-Approval: The Real Deal

Full documentation — pay stubs, tax returns, bank statements, hard credit pull. Lender verifies everything and issues a written commitment letter.

🏆 In competitive markets, sellers often will not consider offers without a pre-approval letter. Always get pre-approved before making offers.

Bottom Line

Use pre-qualification for rough budgeting. Get pre-approved when ready to make offers. Pre-approval expires in 60–90 days, so time it close to your house hunt. Use our Affordability Calculator first to know your budget.
