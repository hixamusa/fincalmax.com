What Is Home Equity and How Can You Use It in 2026?

Home equity is one of the most powerful wealth-building tools for homeowners. Understanding how to use it wisely can transform your finances.

Advertisement · 728×90

What Is Home Equity?

Home equity = Home Value minus Loan Balance. If your home is worth $500k and you owe $320k, you have $180k in equity. Track yours with our Home Equity Calculator.

How Equity Grows

Monthly payments reduce your balance. Home appreciation adds value (US homes average 4–5% per year). Extra principal payments accelerate equity building.

3 Ways to Access Equity

Home Equity Loan (lump sum, fixed rate), HELOC (revolving credit line), or Cash-Out Refinance (replace mortgage with larger one). Each has different costs and risks.

⚠️ Your home is collateral. Only borrow equity for value-adding purposes — never for vacations or consumer spending.

Bottom Line

Equity is powerful — use it strategically and carefully. Never risk your home for unnecessary spending.
