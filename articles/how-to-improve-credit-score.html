How to Improve Your Credit Score for a Mortgage in 2026

A 100-point credit score improvement can save you 0,000 on a 00k loan. Here is exactly how to boost your score before applying.

6 Ways to Improve Your Score

1. Pay down credit card balances below 10% utilization. 2. Never miss a payment — set up autopay. 3. Do not close old accounts. 4. Dispute errors on your report. 5. Avoid new credit 6–12 months before applying. 6. Become an authorized user on a family member card.

⏱️ Timeline: Paying down balances shows results in 30–60 days. Building full credit history takes 6–12 months. Start early.

Bottom Line

Start improving your score 12 months before buying. Even going from 680 to 720 saves $200+/month on your mortgage. Use our Mortgage Calculator to see the difference.
