How Much Down Payment Do I Need in 2026?

You do not need 20% down to buy a home. But how much do you actually need — and how does it affect your monthly payment and total cost?

Impact on Monthly Payment

On a $400,000 home at 6.85%: 3% down ($12k) = $2,553 + $280 PMI. 10% down ($40k) = $2,370 + $180 PMI. 20% down ($80k) = $2,107, no PMI.

💡 Going from 3% to 20% down saves $726/month — that is $8,712 per year. Use our Down Payment Calculator to plan your savings.

Bottom Line

More down payment means lower payment and no PMI. But do not drain your emergency fund to hit 20%. A 10% down with healthy savings is often smarter.
