Editorial Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2026

Our Commitment to Accuracy

FincalMax is committed to providing free, accurate, and trustworthy financial calculators and educational content. Every calculator uses industry-standard formulas identical to those used by banks, mortgage lenders, and financial institutions.

Who Creates Our Content

All calculators and articles on FincalMax are created or reviewed by qualified professionals including:

  • Licensed mortgage loan officers (NMLS)
  • Certified Financial Planners (CFP)
  • Certified Public Accountants (CPA)
  • Real estate finance professionals with 10+ years experience

Our Review Process

Every piece of content goes through a three-step process before publication:

  • Creation: Content is written or built by a qualified financial professional
  • Accuracy Check: All calculations are verified against professional mortgage software
  • Editorial Review: Final review for clarity, completeness, and factual accuracy

Advertising & Independence

FincalMax displays advertising through Google AdSense to fund our free service. Our editorial team operates independently — advertisers have no influence over our calculator formulas, article content, or recommendations.

We do not accept sponsored content, paid placements, or affiliate commissions that influence our recommendations. All calculator results are mathematically generated — never influenced by business relationships.

Corrections Policy

If you find an error in any calculator or article, please contact us immediately. We investigate all reported errors and correct them within 48 hours if confirmed. We believe in transparency and will note corrections prominently.

Content Updates

We review and update our content regularly to reflect current mortgage rates, tax laws, and financial regulations. Articles are dated with their last review date. Calculator default values are updated as market conditions change.

Disclaimer

While we strive for maximum accuracy, FincalMax content is for informational purposes only and does not constitute financial advice. Always consult a licensed professional before making any major financial decision. See our full Disclaimer.
