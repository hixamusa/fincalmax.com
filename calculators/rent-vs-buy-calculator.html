📖 What These Results Mean

This calculator compares the true cost of renting versus buying over the years you plan to stay, factoring in home appreciation, rent increases, and the equity you'd build by owning. The “verdict” shows which option saves you more money — not just which has the lower monthly payment, since buying builds equity that renting doesn't.
