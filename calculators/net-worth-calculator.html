📖 What These Results Mean

Net worth is simply everything you own (assets) minus everything you owe (liabilities) — the single clearest number for tracking your financial progress over time. It's normal for net worth to be low or even negative early in adulthood (student loans, a new car loan); what matters is the trend over months and years, not the snapshot today.
