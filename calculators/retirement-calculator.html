📖 What These Results Mean

This compares two numbers: what you're projected to actually have saved by retirement (based on your current trajectory) versus the nest egg you'd need to fund your desired income for your expected years in retirement. If you're short, increasing your monthly contribution even modestly — or working a couple years longer — closes the gap faster than you'd expect thanks to compounding.
