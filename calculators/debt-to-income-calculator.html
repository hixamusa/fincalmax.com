📖 What These Results Mean

Lenders use your debt-to-income ratio (DTI) — your monthly debt payments divided by your gross monthly income — to decide how much they'll lend you. Enter your income, existing debts, and the housing payment you're considering. A DTI under 36% is considered excellent; most lenders cap conventional loans around 43-45%.
