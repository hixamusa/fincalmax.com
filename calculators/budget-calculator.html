📖 What These Results Mean

The 50/30/20 rule is a simple budgeting guideline: 50% of your after-tax income toward needs (rent, groceries, utilities), 30% toward wants (dining out, entertainment, subscriptions), and 20% toward savings or paying down debt. It's a starting point, not a strict rule — high cost-of-living areas often need to flex the needs percentage higher.
