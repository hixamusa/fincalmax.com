📖 What These Results Mean

Home equity is simply your home's value minus what you still owe on it — and it's often a homeowner's biggest source of borrowing power. Enter your home's current value and your remaining mortgage balance to see your total equity and how much you could likely borrow through a home equity loan or HELOC (most lenders cap you at 80% of your home's value).
