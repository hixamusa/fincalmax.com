📖 What These Results Mean

Net cash flow is simply what's left over each month after rent collects and every expense — mortgage, taxes, insurance, maintenance, vacancy, and management — gets paid. A positive number means the property is profitable month to month; if it's negative, you're subsidizing the property out of your own pocket.
