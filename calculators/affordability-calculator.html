📖 What These Results Mean

This calculator works backward from your income and debts to tell you the maximum home price you can comfortably afford, using the same 28%/36% guideline most lenders use. Enter your income, monthly debts, and the down payment you have saved. If your max price feels too low, paying down debt or increasing your down payment are the two biggest levers you can pull.
